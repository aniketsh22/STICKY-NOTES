  • CLICK ON THE ADD NOTE BUTTON TO ADD YOUR NOTE
  • A WRITE NOTE POP-UP BOX WILL OPEN, WHERE YOU CAN WRITE YOUR NOTES
  • AFTER WRITING YOUR NOTE CLICK ON THE [ TICK ] BUTTON TO SAVE YOUR NOTE
  • IF YOU DON'T WANT OT SAVE YOUR NOTE CLICK ON THE [ X ] BUTTON
  • ALL YOUR SAVE NOTES WILL APPEAR ON THE SCREEN
  • IF YOU WANT TO DELETE THE SAVED NOTE JUST DOUBLE CLICK ON THAT PARTICULAR NOTE, HENCE THE NOTE WILL BE DELETED
